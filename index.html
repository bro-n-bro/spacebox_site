what is spacebox?
Spacebox is a comprehensive set of open-source tools for data indexation and storage, utilizing the cutting-edge technology of ClickHouse as the foundation for the storage facility. It's built to provide quick access to large data sets and ensure a stable architecture that guarantees data consistency and enables a lightweight setup.

Spacebox in Use

Spacebox offers a suite of developer tools and analytics tailored for building applications on Cosmos-based blockchains.

Developers can access comprehensive data indexing to facilitate smart contract development, transaction monitoring, and performance optimization.

DeFi projects and applications built on Cosmos-based blockchains can benefit from Spacebox's indexing capabilities to access critical data on liquidity pools, token swaps, and yield farming opportunities.

Spacebox provides the infrastructure necessary for DeFi innovation, empowering developers to create decentralized financial products and services within the Cosmos ecosystem.

Spacebox is designed for a diverse range of users within the Cosmos ecosystem, including developers building on Cosmos-based blockchains, traders and investors seeking market insights, governance participants involved in network decision-making, researchers and educators studying blockchain technology, DeFi projects and applications leveraging Cosmos infrastructure, and community members engaging in collaboration and innovation.
