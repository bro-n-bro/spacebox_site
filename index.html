what is spacebox?
Comprehensive set of
open-source tools for data indexation and storage
Spacebox is a powerful blockchain indexing tool designed to help users explore and access the data within the Cosmos blockchain ecosystem. It efficiently indexes and organizes blockchain transactions, smart contracts, tokens, and more, providing users with a clear and accessible way to navigate and utilize blockchain data within the Cosmos network. It serves as your key to unlocking the insights hidden within the Cosmos blockchain.
